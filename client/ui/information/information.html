Sandbox - Developed by Digital. Maintained by Logic.

World saves to world.json.

Press P to toggle editor.

Double LMB to select/ deselct objects.

Press F to teleport player to cursor.

Press G to fire firework on cursor.

Press LALT to switch edit mode.

Press F10 to hide UI & chat.

Use SCROLL to speed up/down the camera.

Use SHIFT + S to save.

Use SHIFT + C to dupe.

Use PGUP/ PGDOWN to +/- height.

Use /objectspeed to change speed.
